About me

I have spent most of my educational years studying the Talmud in depth, which is analytical thinking at its core. It then came time for me to focus on what I would like to do for a career. Having always enjoyed logical thinking, I felt that software engineering would be a good fit. I consulted with acquaintances who work in the tech industry to find out what it is all about, and I was very excited with what I heard.

I then earned a BS in Software Development from Bellevue University. Having completed my degree and having learned the foundations of Java programming, I was hungry to learn more and explore additional areas of software development, while being able to apply those skills in a practical manner. Someone suggested that I enroll in a boot-camp to hone my practical skills and learn the frameworks currently used in the industry

I completed the LearningFuze boot-camp in Jan '21. The number of additional skills I have picked up is astounding. Although the instructors at Learning Fuze are extremely supportive and always willing to help, the program is designed to teach students how to learn and solve problems on their own. Additionally, throughout the program we have been given projects both large and small to put our knowledge to test. This has helped me tremendously through accustoming me to constantly come up with solutions to problems on hand.

With my recent web development experience at Learning Fuze, plus Bachelor’s degree in Software Development, I am confident that it would make me a great asset to your team. I would love for the opportunity to bring my passion and dedication to coding to your company.
